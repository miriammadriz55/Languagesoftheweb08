Miley Cyrus an iconic music artist


Miley Cyrus' original name is Destiny Hope Cyrus, she was born in Tennessee in 1992. Her career skyrocketed when she stared in the hit series Hannah Montana on Disney Chanel. The soundtrack for the show was sung by her and that shot her into being a star. She is the daughter of famous country singer Biley Ray Cyrus. She started playing Hannah Montana in 2005, a show about a teenage girl living a double life. One life being an average american teenager and the other of being a pop-singer star named Hannah. In 2007 she toured for the album produced by the show called "Best of both worlds". After that she gained a lot more following. In 2013 she left her stage name of Hannah Montana and started singing with her own name and droped the album bangerz. She has since dropped many albums, she recently dropped an album called plastic hearts in 2020.
